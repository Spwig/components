LinkedIn Company Page Setup Instructions

Prerequisites

  • A LinkedIn Company Page that you administer
  • A LinkedIn Developer account
  • Marketing Developer Platform access (for organization posting)

Step 1: Create a LinkedIn App

  1. Go to LinkedIn Developers
  2. Click "Create app"
  3. Fill in your app details:
    • App name
    • LinkedIn Page (associate with your company page)
    • App logo
  4. Accept the terms and create the app

Step 2: Request Marketing Developer Platform Access

  1. In your app settings, go to the "Products" tab
  2. Request access to "Marketing Developer Platform"
  3. Fill out the required information about your use case
  4. Wait for approval (usually 1-3 business days)

Note: Organization posting requires Marketing Developer Platform access.

Step 3: Configure OAuth 2.0

  1. Go to the "Auth" tab in your app settings
  2. Add the following Authorized redirect URL:
{redirect_uri}

Step 4: Request Required OAuth Scopes

Your app needs the following OAuth 2.0 scopes:

  • r_organization_social - Read organization social data
  • w_organization_social - Post on behalf of an organization
  • rw_organization_admin - Manage organization pages

Request these in the "OAuth 2.0 scopes" section of your app settings.

Step 5: Get Your App Credentials

  1. Go to the "Auth" tab
  2. Copy your Client ID and Client Secret
  3. Add these to your platform's environment variables:
    • LINKEDIN_CLIENT_ID
    • LINKEDIN_CLIENT_SECRET

Step 6: Verify Your Company Page

  1. Go to the "Products" tab
  2. Under "Marketing Developer Platform", click "Verify"
  3. Select your LinkedIn Company Page to verify
  4. Complete the verification process

Step 7: Connect Your Account

  1. Click "Authorize" below to start the OAuth flow
  2. Log in with your LinkedIn account
  3. Grant the requested permissions
  4. Select which Organization to connect
