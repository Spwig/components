Instagram Business Setup Instructions

Prerequisites

  • An Instagram Business or Creator account
  • A Facebook Page connected to your Instagram account
  • Admin access to both the Facebook Page and Instagram account

Step 1: Create a Facebook App

  1. Go to developers.facebook.com
  2. Click "My Apps" and then "Create App"
  3. Select "Business" as the app type
  4. Fill in the app details and create the app

Step 2: Configure Instagram API

  1. In your app dashboard, click "Add Product"
  2. Find "Instagram" and click "Set Up"
  3. Complete the Instagram Basic Display setup if prompted
  4. Navigate to "Instagram > Basic Display" in the sidebar

Step 3: Configure OAuth Settings

  1. Go to "Facebook Login" > "Settings"
  2. Add the following Valid OAuth Redirect URI:
{redirect_uri}

Step 4: Request Permissions

Your app needs the following permissions:

  • instagram_basic - Read Instagram account info
  • instagram_content_publish - Publish content to Instagram
  • pages_show_list - List Facebook Pages
  • pages_read_engagement - Read page engagement data

Note: Some permissions require App Review before going live.

Step 5: Get Your App Credentials

  1. Go to "Settings" > "Basic"
  2. Copy your App ID and App Secret
  3. Add these to your platform's environment variables:
    • FACEBOOK_APP_ID
    • FACEBOOK_APP_SECRET

Step 6: Connect Your Instagram Account

  1. Click "Authorize" below to start the OAuth flow
  2. Log in with your Facebook account
  3. Grant the requested permissions
  4. Select which Instagram Business account to connect
