Setting up Square Terminal

  1. Get a Square account — Sign up at squareup.com/signup
  2. Create an application — In the Square Developer Dashboard, create a new application.
  3. Get your access token — In the application settings, find your sandbox or production access token. Ensure it has PAYMENTS_WRITE, PAYMENTS_READ, and DEVICES_READ permissions.
  4. Note your location ID — In the Square Dashboard, go to Account & Settings > Business information > Locations and copy the location ID.
  5. Set up your terminal — Power on your Square Terminal and follow the on-screen instructions to connect to WiFi and sign in to your Square account.
  6. Test the connection — Use the "Test Connection" button in the POS Terminal Provider settings to verify API connectivity.

Supported Devices

  • Square Terminal — All-in-one countertop terminal with touchscreen and built-in receipt printer
  • Square Reader — Compact contactless and chip card reader

Sandbox Testing

Square provides a sandbox environment with test device IDs for development. Use the sandbox environment setting and sandbox access token during development. Switch to production for live payments.
