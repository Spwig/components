Revolut Payment Setup

Why Choose Revolut? Accept card payments, Revolut Pay, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Pay by Bank with competitive rates. No monthly fees, fast settlement, and a seamless checkout experience.

Step 1: Create Your Revolut Business Account

  1. Visit business.revolut.com/signup
  2. Sign up for a Revolut Business account (Grow plan or above)
  3. Complete the registration and identity verification
  4. Enable Merchant functionality in your business account settings
Note: You need a Revolut Business account with Merchant access enabled. Personal accounts cannot accept payments.

Step 2: Generate API Keys

  1. Log into your Revolut Business Dashboard
  2. Navigate to Developer → API Keys
  3. Click "Generate API Keys"
  4. Copy your Secret Key (starts with sk_live_ or sk_test_)
  5. Copy your Public Key (starts with pk_live_ or pk_test_)
Security: Never share your Secret Key. Keep it secure and don't commit it to version control.

Step 3: Configure Webhook (Recommended)

  1. In the Revolut Business Dashboard, go to Developer → Webhooks
  2. Click "Add Webhook"
  3. Enter your webhook URL:
  4. Select the following events:
    • ORDER_COMPLETED
    • ORDER_AUTHORISED
    • ORDER_CANCELLED
    • ORDER_FAILED
    • ORDER_PAYMENT_AUTHENTICATED
    • ORDER_PAYMENT_DECLINED
    • ORDER_PAYMENT_FAILED
  5. Save and copy the Signing Secret provided
Why Webhooks? Webhooks ensure your orders are updated in real-time when payments are completed, cancelled, or fail.

Step 4: Enter Your Credentials Below

  1. Paste your Secret Key
  2. Paste your Public Key (optional, for embedded checkout)
  3. Paste the Webhook Signing Secret (if configured)
  4. Choose your Environment:
    • Sandbox: For testing with simulated payments
    • Production: For real transactions
  5. Select your preferred Capture Mode:
    • Automatic: Payments captured immediately
    • Manual: You capture payments later (useful for physical goods)
  6. Click "Test Connection" to verify

Step 5: Test Your Integration

Testing Mode: Use the Sandbox environment to test payments without real money.

Test Cards:

  • Success: 4929 4212 3445 1236 (any future expiry, CVV: 123)
  • 3D Secure: 2720 9933 2245 6015 (triggers 3DS challenge)
  • Declined: 5431 3002 0042 2415 (simulates declined payment)

Visit Revolut Testing Guide for more test scenarios.

Step 6: Go Live

  1. Complete all Revolut KYC and business verification
  2. Test thoroughly in Sandbox mode first
  3. Switch Environment to Production
  4. Generate production API keys and update your configuration
  5. Process a small test transaction to verify
