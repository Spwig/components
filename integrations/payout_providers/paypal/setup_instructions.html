PayPal Payouts API Setup

Follow these steps to obtain your PayPal API credentials for processing affiliate payouts.

1

Create or Log In to PayPal Developer Account

Visit developer.paypal.com and log in with your PayPal Business account credentials.

You must have a PayPal Business account to use the Payouts API. Personal accounts do not support payouts.

2

Navigate to Apps & Credentials

From the Developer Dashboard, click on Apps & Credentials in the left sidebar.

Select either Sandbox (for testing) or Live (for production) depending on your needs.

3

Create a New App

Click Create App and enter a name for your application (e.g., "Store Affiliate Payouts").

Select your Business account as the associated PayPal account.

4

Enable Payouts Feature

In your app settings, scroll down to Features and ensure Payouts is enabled.

The Payouts feature requires approval from PayPal. If not available, contact PayPal support to enable it for your account.

5

Copy Your API Credentials

From your app details page, copy the following:

  • Client ID - Your app's unique identifier
  • Client Secret - Click "Show" to reveal and copy (keep this secure)
6

Set Up Webhooks (Optional but Recommended)

To receive real-time payout status updates:

  1. In your app settings, scroll to Webhooks
  2. Click Add Webhook
  3. Enter your webhook URL: {{ webhook_url }}
  4. Select these events:
    • PAYMENT.PAYOUTSBATCH.SUCCESS
    • PAYMENT.PAYOUTSBATCH.DENIED
    • PAYMENT.PAYOUTS-ITEM.SUCCEEDED
    • PAYMENT.PAYOUTS-ITEM.FAILED
    • PAYMENT.PAYOUTS-ITEM.UNCLAIMED
  5. After creating, copy the Webhook ID for signature verification
7

Fund Your Account

Ensure your PayPal Business account has sufficient balance to process payouts.

PayPal charges a fee of 2% per payout (capped at $20 USD or equivalent).

Need Help? Visit PayPal Payouts API Documentation for detailed technical information.
