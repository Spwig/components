This guide will help you set up Twilio to send SMS messages from your store. Twilio is a cloud communications platform that provides reliable global SMS delivery.

Time Required: 5-10 minutes
Prerequisites: None (free trial available)

Quick Setup Overview

To use Twilio SMS, you'll need:

  1. A Twilio account (free trial or paid)
  2. Account SID and Auth Token from your dashboard
  3. A Twilio phone number for sending messages

Step-by-Step Setup

1

Create a Twilio Account

  1. Go to Twilio Sign Up
  2. Enter your email, name, and create a password
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Verify your phone number (for security)
Free Trial: New accounts receive $15 in free trial credit to test SMS sending.
2

Get Your Account SID and Auth Token

  1. Log in to your Twilio Console
  2. On the dashboard, locate your Account SID and Auth Token
  3. Click the "eye" icon next to Auth Token to reveal it
  4. Copy both values - you'll need them in the next step
Keep Your Auth Token Secret!

Never share your Auth Token publicly or commit it to version control. Anyone with your Account SID and Auth Token can send messages using your account.

3

Get a Twilio Phone Number

  1. In the Console, go to Phone Numbers → Manage → Buy a number
  2. Or use the link: Buy a Phone Number
  3. Select your country and search for available numbers
  4. Choose a number with SMS capability (check the SMS column)
  5. Click Buy to purchase the number
Free Trial Numbers: During the free trial, you'll get a free phone number with limited capabilities. Upgrade to unlock full features.
4

Enter Your Credentials

Use the following information to configure the SMS provider:

Field Where to Find It
Account SID Twilio Console Dashboard (starts with AC)
Auth Token Twilio Console Dashboard (click eye icon to reveal)
From Phone Number Your Twilio phone number in E.164 format (e.g., +15551234567)
5

Test Your Connection

After entering your credentials, click the Test Connection button to verify everything is working correctly.

A successful test will show:

  • Your account name and status
  • Whether your phone number is valid

Free Trial Limitations

During the free trial, Twilio has some restrictions:

  • Can only send to verified phone numbers (numbers you've added to your account)
  • Messages include "Sent from Twilio trial account" prefix
  • Limited to the trial credit balance

To remove these limitations, upgrade to a paid account in the Twilio Console.

Verifying Phone Numbers (Trial Accounts)

If you're on a trial account and need to send to specific numbers:

  1. Go to Verified Caller IDs
  2. Click Add a new Caller ID
  3. Enter the phone number you want to text
  4. Verify via SMS or voice call

Troubleshooting

Error: "Authentication Error"

  • Verify your Account SID starts with AC
  • Make sure you copied the Auth Token correctly (it's hidden by default)
  • Check that you're using the main account credentials, not API key credentials

Error: "Phone number is not valid"

  • Ensure the phone number is in E.164 format: +[country code][number]
  • Verify the number is associated with your Twilio account
  • Check that the number has SMS capability

Error: "Cannot send to unverified number"

  • This occurs on trial accounts
  • Either verify the recipient number or upgrade to a paid account

Messages Not Delivered

  • Check the SMS Logs in your Twilio Console
  • Look for error codes and details
  • Common issues: invalid recipient number, carrier filtering, daily limits
